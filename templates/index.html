About Trunow Technologies

Established in 2016, Trunow Technologies Pvt Ltd is a leading provider of enterprise-grade IT infrastructure and cabling solutions. We specialize in data cabling, fiber optic installations, network infrastructure setup, and security systems.

Our team brings over 20 years of combined experience in delivering end-to-end infrastructure solutions. From initial consultation to design, implementation, testing, and ongoing support, we ensure seamless project execution with fixed pricing and no hidden costs.
